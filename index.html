Stay one step ahead

Peebeep is a device designed to detect cancer by tracking urine, giving you control over your health

Why chosing PeeBeep?

With our technology, you keep your health in check without thinking about it. You can stop worrying about your next consultation, and instead, check yourself without leaving your house. Keep on leaving normally while our device does the rest.

Non-invasive method

Forget about painful and uncomfortable procedures - with Peebeep's non-invasive urine monitoring technology, you can now track your health status easily and comfortably. .

Early detection

Early detection of lung cancer is crucial for taking timely action. About 57% of patients get diagnosed at a late stage due to the lack of noticeable symptoms early on. Peebeep technology ensures this will not happen.

Passive

All you need to do is use the toilet and the device will take care of the rest. You will forget the device is there unless health concerns appear that require your attention.

At-home diagnosis

Peebeep allows you to track your health at home and gives you the peace of mind that comes with knowing you are taking care of your health.

How does it work?

Peebeep is very easy to use!

1

Installation

You can easily install Peebeep by following the step-by-step instruction guide provided with the device. Additionally, you can connect it to your smartphone using Bluetooth for clearer status notifications and you're good to go.

2

Daily use

Peebeep requires no additional effort during your daily toilet routine. Just use the toilet as you normally would, in the meantime the technology will monitor your urine for any abnormalities or changes.

3

Potential detection

If Peebeep detects anything unusual, the device will start beeping to alert you and a notification will be sent to your registered phone. The notification will provide you with details about the potential issue and what actions you should take.
