Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

How often do I need to clean Peebeep?

Peebeep is a self-cleaning device, which means it does not require any manual cleaning. The device uses advanced technology to ensure that it always remains clean and hygienic. This makes it a convenient and hassle-free way to monitor your health without any extra maintenance.

Is Peebeep difficult to install?

Installing Peebeep is very easy, and it can be done quickly and without any special tools. Simply follow the instructions provided, and you'll be up and running in no time.

Will it work with my toilet?

The device is designed to work with any type of toilet, so you don't need to worry about compatibility issues. 

How noticeable is it? And how much noise does it make?

The device is small and discreet, and it can be easily installed out of sight. Once installed, you can forget about it, and it will work quietly and efficiently in the background.
